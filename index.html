About Our Lodge

Welcome to Lukundo Sichalwe Lodge

Nestled in a serene natural setting, Lukundo Sichalwe Lodge has been providing exceptional hospitality since 2025. Our lodge is named after the renowned local conservationist Lukundo Sichalwe, who dedicated his life to preserving the natural beauty of this region.

We offer a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional charm, with each space thoughtfully designed to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that your stay contributes to the preservation of the local environment and community.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, our attentive staff and premium amenities will make your stay truly memorable.
